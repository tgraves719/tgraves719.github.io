About us

Portraits of Change is an ongoing music-technology project that aims to cultivate jazz music through bold and unabashed innovation. It was created and developed by jazz musician and music student Thomas Graves. More information, including a full roster of artists and bios can be found here.

Questions? Suggestions? Comments? Email us and follow us on social media to stay up to date!
